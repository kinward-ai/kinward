GETTING STARTED

From download to first conversation

Total time: 5–10 minutes (including AI model download)

1
Download Kinward
~30 seconds
📥
Get the code You

Option A — If you have Git:

> git clone https://github.com/kinward-ai/kinward
> cd kinward

Option B — No Git? No problem:

Go to github.com/kinward-ai/kinward → click the green Code button → Download ZIP. Unzip it anywhere you want.

2
Run the Start Script
~1–3 minutes (first time only)
▶️
One command You

Open the Kinward folder and double-click start.bat (Windows) or run ./start.sh (Mac/Linux) in Terminal.

That's it. The script does everything else.

🧰
Auto-installs what's missing Automatic

The script checks for everything Kinward needs. If something is missing, it tells you and asks to install it. Just type Y for yes.

[3/4] Installing dependencies...
[4/4] Launching Kinward...

Kinward is starting up!
The browser will open automatically.
Tip: On the first run, installing dependencies takes about a minute. After that, startup takes just a few seconds.
3
Setup Wizard
~2–3 minutes
🔮
The wizard walks you through everything You

Your browser opens automatically. The setup wizard guides you through 6 quick steps:

STEP 1
Welcome
Quick intro, one button to start
STEP 2
System Check
Auto-detects your hardware
STEP 3
Privacy Mode
Choose Open, Secured, or Lockdown
STEP 4
Your Profile
Create your name and 4-digit PIN
STEP 5
Family
Add family members (or skip)
STEP 6
AI Models
Pick and install your AI brain
About model downloads: In Step 6, Kinward recommends the best AI model for your hardware. Downloading a model is a one-time thing — it ranges from 2GB to 5GB depending on the model. This is usually the longest part of setup. After that, everything runs locally with no internet needed.
4
Start Chatting
You're done!
💬
Your family's AI is live Ready

Tap your name, enter your PIN, pick a conversation mode, and start talking. Everything stays on your machine — no cloud, no accounts, no data leaving your home.

📱
Connect from any device

Everyone in the house can connect from their phone, tablet, or laptop. Just open a browser and go to the address shown in the terminal (something like http://192.168.1.50:5173). On your phone, tap "Add to Home Screen" for an app-like experience.

📚
Things to try

Upload a document — drag a PDF, text file, or photo into the chat. Ask questions about it. Key facts are remembered automatically.

Teach it about your family — mention allergies, school schedules, pet names. It extracts and stores these facts for future conversations.

Name your AI — go to Settings and give your assistant a custom name, personality, and tagline.

Try different modes — switch between General, Kids, Research, and Creative for different conversation styles.

?
Troubleshooting
Common questions

"The browser didn't open" — Open your browser manually and go to http://localhost:5173

"npm install failed" — The script will tell you what's missing and offer to install it. If it still fails, check your internet connection and try again.

"It says Ollama isn't running" — Open a new terminal and run ollama serve, then refresh the page.

"The AI is slow" — Smaller models run faster. In Settings, try switching to a lighter model. A GPU makes a big difference — even a budget one.

Found a bug? — Email hello@kinward.ai or open an issue on GitHub.
